About Us

Who We Are

Blessed Secondary School is a premier educational institution in Lusada, Ogun State. Founded on the pillars of excellence, integrity, and character, we have consistently produced outstanding students who make a mark in Nigerian society and beyond.

We offer both Junior Secondary (JSS 1–3) and Senior Secondary (SS 1–3) programmes. Our students sit for WAEC and NECO examinations with outstanding results, and our 98% pass rate speaks for itself.
