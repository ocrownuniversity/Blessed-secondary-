About Us

Who We Are

Blessed Secondary School is a leading educational institution in Lusada, Ogun State, built on the foundations of academic excellence, strong moral values, and holistic character development.

We offer a complete secondary education from Junior Secondary (JSS 1–3) to Senior Secondary (SS 1–3), preparing our students for WAEC, NECO, and beyond — with a 98% pass rate that speaks for itself.

Our modern facilities, dedicated teachers, and vibrant school community create an environment where every student thrives academically, socially, and morally.
