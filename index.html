About Us

Who We Are

Blessed Secondary School is a premier educational institution located in Lusada, Ogun State, Nigeria. Founded with a vision to provide world-class secondary education, we have consistently delivered academic excellence combined with strong character formation.

We offer both Junior Secondary (JSS 1–3) and Senior Secondary (SS 1–3) programmes, preparing students for WAEC and NECO examinations with outstanding results year after year.

Our dedicated team of qualified teachers, modern facilities, and innovative teaching methods create an environment where every student can discover and maximise their potential.

Our Curriculum

A comprehensive Nigerian curriculum meeting WAEC and NECO standards, preparing students thoroughly for external examinations and university entrance.

Our JSS programme covers Mathematics, English, Basic Science, Basic Technology, Social Studies, CRS/IRS, French, Yoruba, and Physical Education. The SS programme offers Science, Commercial, and Art streams leading to WAEC/NECO.
